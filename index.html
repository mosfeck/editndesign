From the President

Message from President

Welcome to Dhaka Chamber of Commerce and Industry (DCCI) where we firmly believe that “The Best of Bangladesh is Business”. DCCI is a place where ideas are incubated regarding development of trade and business towards the ultimate goal of an economically prosperous Bangladesh.

DCCI is the first point of contact for business in this country. It facilitates trade and commerce for both local and foreign entrepreneurs catering to their demands and requirements. DCCI is a limited company incorporated in 1958 under the companies Act 1913 (amended in 1994).

Latest News

  • 28 Jun 2018
Speakers urged for knowledge dissemination of Bangladesh GAP

Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry (DCCI) in cooperation with USAID’s Agriculture Value Chain (AVC) Project organized a seminar on “Knowledge Dissemination on Bangladesh GAP”. The meaning of GAP is Good Agricultural Practice. Good Agricultural Practice (GAP) is an internationally practiced method to ensure safe and sustainable agriculture production. GAP is a set of principles, regulations and technical recommendations applicable to production, processing and transporting addressing human health care, environment protection and improvement of working conditions.

  • 13 May 2018
Regulatory barriers impeding the growth of warehousing sector

“Regulatory barriers are impeding the growth of warehousing sector in Bangladesh” speakers said in a workshop organized by Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry (DCCI) supported by International Finance Corporation (IFC) on 13th May, 2018 at Sonargaon Hotel, Dhaka. The World Bank Group commissioned a study on "Bangladesh logistics costs study: assessment of storage and warehousing" to analyze current and future industry potential and estimate the market size of warehousing market in Bangladesh. Secretary, Ministry of Shipping, GoB Md. Abdus Samad was present as the chief guest.

  • 12 May 2018
Finance Minister hints corporate tax cut in a pre-budget discussion

Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry (DCCI), The Daily Samakal and Chanel 24 jointly organized a "Pre-budget Discussion" at Bangabandhu International Conference Centre (BICC) on 12th May, 2018. Finance Minister Abul Maal Abdul Muhith, MP was present as the chief guest. The programme was moderated by Golam Sarwar, Editor, The Daily Samakal which was telecast live in Chanel 24.DCCI President Abul Kasem Khan in his welcome address said we want that the upcoming budget will focus on 4Es that is Empowerment, Encouragement, Engagement and Environment. He also termed for some priority areas like policy reforms, simplification of taxation system
